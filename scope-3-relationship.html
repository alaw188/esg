Asset Design is Our Destiny

If we manufacture and lease equipment that can *only* burn dirty diesel, we lock ourselves into high downstream Scope 3 footprints for decades. Hybrid or multi-fuel compatibility is key.

📋

"Green Lease" Frameworks

We can offer financial incentives or contract structures that bundle green fuel access (like biodiesel or electric charging credits) to make decarbonization easier for our clients.

🤝

Joint Scope Reduction

A client switching to green energy immediately lowers their Scope 1 direct operations while simultaneously vaporizing our Scope 3 liability. It is a true mutual benefit.
